## Why Archive?

These documents are preserved for:
- **Historical context** - Understanding past decisions and evolution
- **Learning** - Reviewing completed investigations and evaluations
- **Reference** - Looking up details from earlier phases
- **Continuity** - Maintaining full project history

## When to Add to Archive

Move documents here when they:
- Represent completed work that won't be updated
- Are superseded by newer documentation
- Contain historical snapshots or evaluations
- Are session-specific handoff documents from concluded sessions
